Little About Us

Aroma Ice Cream is one of the fastest growing ice cream companies in the country. It is known for its quality product innovation. We are committed to delivering excellent product quality with a homemade taste which distinguishes us from the other commercial brands.

Under the supervision of an efficient team comprising food experts, nutritionists, quality controllers, sales personnel and administrative staff we are able to perform pasteurization, homogenization, hardening, ageing the mix, freezing and packaging at our facilities.

Aroma Ice Cream Makes a wide variety of Speciality Flavours & seasonal Flavours.
